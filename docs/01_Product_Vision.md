# Career Scout AI – Product Vision

## Vision

Career Scout AI is an AI-powered job discovery platform designed to help experienced professionals efficiently discover relevant career opportunities across multiple company career portals.

Instead of manually visiting dozens of career websites every day, users receive a curated, personalized list of newly posted roles that align with their skills, experience, and career aspirations.

The long-term vision is to create an intelligent career assistant that continuously monitors the job market, understands a candidate's profile, and proactively surfaces high-value opportunities before they are missed.

---

# Problem Statement

Searching for jobs across multiple company career portals is repetitive, time-consuming, and inefficient.

Professionals targeting product and program management roles often monitor careers pages from companies such as Microsoft, Google, Amazon, JPMorgan Chase, and other organizations because these positions may appear on company websites before they are widely distributed on job boards.

Current challenges include:

* Repeatedly checking multiple career websites.
* Missing newly posted opportunities.
* Reading hundreds of irrelevant job descriptions.
* Managing duplicate postings across different sources.
* Difficulty prioritizing roles based on individual experience.

This manual process reduces productivity and increases the risk of missing suitable opportunities.

---

# Product Goal

Build an intelligent platform that automatically:

* Monitors selected company career pages.
* Detects newly published job postings.
* Eliminates duplicate listings.
* Filters jobs based on user-defined preferences.
* Scores each opportunity based on profile relevance.
* Notifies users only when high-value opportunities are identified.

---

# Target Users

### Primary Users

Experienced professionals seeking Product Management, Program Management, Strategy, and FinTech leadership roles.

Typical user profile:

* 10–20 years of industry experience
* Interested in product, platform, or strategy roles
* Tracks multiple company career portals
* Applies selectively to high-quality opportunities

---

# User Problems

Users need a way to:

* Stop manually checking career pages.
* Receive only relevant job alerts.
* Quickly determine whether a role is worth applying for.
* Track opportunities from multiple companies in one place.
* Reduce the time spent searching while increasing the quality of applications.

---

# Proposed Solution

Career Scout AI provides an automated pipeline that:

1. Scans configured career portals on a scheduled interval.
2. Extracts newly published job postings.
3. Detects duplicate jobs.
4. Matches jobs against predefined keywords and preferences.
5. Calculates a relevance score.
6. Presents ranked opportunities through APIs and a dashboard.
7. Sends notifications when highly relevant jobs are found.

---

# Success Metrics

The product will be considered successful if it can:

* Reduce manual job search effort.
* Detect newly posted roles within a configurable time window.
* Minimize duplicate notifications.
* Improve the relevance of recommended jobs.
* Provide a reliable, scalable platform for continuous monitoring.

# Product Principles

Career Scout AI is guided by the following principles:

* User value over technical complexity.
* Automation before manual effort.
* Relevance over volume.
* Simple APIs and modular architecture.
* Incremental delivery through small, measurable MVPs.
* Transparent decision-making backed by documented trade-offs.
