# ai-job-search-agent
Why Career Scout AI?

Experienced professionals often monitor multiple company career pages to discover new opportunities.

This process is repetitive, fragmented, and time-consuming. While platforms like LinkedIn aggregate job listings, professionals targeting specific organizations frequently revisit individual career portals to identify newly posted roles, validate role details, and avoid missing high-value opportunities.

Career Scout AI is designed to automate this workflow by continuously monitoring selected company career pages, identifying newly published roles, evaluating their relevance, and surfacing personalized recommendations through an API-first platform.

The Problem

Job searching today presents several challenges for experienced professionals:

Monitoring multiple company career pages requires repetitive manual effort.
Newly published opportunities can be missed between periodic searches.
Job boards often return a high volume of results with varying levels of relevance.
Evaluating job descriptions against individual experience consumes significant time.
Tracking opportunities across multiple employers lacks a unified workflow.
Proposed Solution

Career Scout AI automates job discovery by:

Monitoring selected company career pages.
Detecting newly published job postings.
Eliminating duplicate listings.
Matching opportunities against user preferences.
Ranking opportunities using configurable scoring and, in later phases, AI-assisted relevance.
Delivering timely notifications through APIs and future user interfaces.
Product Goals

The project aims to:

Reduce manual effort involved in job discovery.
Improve the relevance of recommended opportunities.
Enable users to focus on high-quality applications instead of repetitive searching.
Demonstrate an API-first architecture that can scale from individual users to enterprise scenarios.
Target Users

Career Scout AI is initially designed for experienced professionals pursuing:

Product Management
Program Management
Product Strategy
Technical Program Management
FinTech Leadership
Platform and API Product roles

The architecture is intentionally extensible to support additional roles and industries.
